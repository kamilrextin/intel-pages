MARKETING AUTOMATION COMPARISON

HubSpot vs Marketo vs Pardot

Which marketing automation platform is right for your CRM and company stage?

Updated April 2026 Based on 100+ implementations

TL;DR - The Quick Answer

HubSpot = All-in-one with native CRM. Easiest to use. Best for SMB/mid-market. $800-$3,600/mo.

Marketo = Enterprise-grade power. Most customizable. Deep SFDC integration. $1,500-$4,500/mo.

Pardot = Avoid. Outdated UX, limited features, painful to use. We actively migrate clients off it.

Pick HubSpot for 90% of cases, Marketo for true enterprise complexity. Don't pick Pardot.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between HubSpot, Marketo, and Pardot? +

HubSpot is an all-in-one platform with native CRM, best for SMB to mid-market companies seeking simplicity. Marketo (now Adobe Marketo Engage) is the most powerful enterprise-grade platform for complex, multi-channel campaigns and deep Salesforce integration. Pardot (now Marketing Cloud Account Engagement) is Salesforce's native marketing automation, ideal for Salesforce-first companies. HubSpot costs $800-$3,600/mo, Marketo $1,500-$4,500/mo, and Pardot $1,250-$4,000/mo.

Is HubSpot better than Marketo? +

It depends on your needs. HubSpot is better for ease of use, faster time-to-value, and all-in-one functionality with its native CRM. It's ideal for companies under $50M ARR or those without dedicated marketing operations staff. Marketo is better for enterprise complexity, advanced lead scoring, multi-touch attribution, and deep Salesforce integration. Choose HubSpot for simplicity and speed; choose Marketo for power and customization.

Should I use Pardot if I have Salesforce? +

Pardot (Marketing Cloud Account Engagement) offers the tightest Salesforce integration since it's built natively on the Salesforce platform. However, it's not automatically the best choice. Consider Pardot if: you're deeply invested in Salesforce, need seamless bi-directional sync, and want Einstein AI scoring. Consider HubSpot or Marketo if: you need more advanced marketing features, better email design tools, or multi-CRM flexibility. Many Salesforce customers successfully use HubSpot or Marketo with Salesforce sync.

How much does Marketo cost in 2026? +

Marketo pricing in 2026 typically ranges from $1,500 to $4,500+ per month depending on database size and features. The Select tier starts at $1,500/mo for up to 10K contacts. Prime tier runs $2,500-$3,500/mo with advanced analytics. Ultimate tier costs $4,000-$4,500+/mo with full attribution and AI features. Implementation typically adds $10K-$30K upfront. Multi-year contracts with Adobe often include 10-15% discounts.

Which marketing automation platform is easiest to use? +

HubSpot is widely considered the easiest marketing automation platform to use. It features intuitive drag-and-drop builders, in-app guidance, and a unified interface for all marketing tools. Pardot is moderately easy to use but requires Salesforce knowledge. Marketo has the steepest learning curve due to its enterprise complexity and requires dedicated admin time. For ease of use ranking: HubSpot > Pardot > Marketo. However, Marketo's complexity enables more powerful automation for those who master it.
