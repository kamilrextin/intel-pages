Behavior: Visited pricing page+15 pts
Behavior: Downloaded case study+10 pts
Intent: Demo request+30 pts
Negative: Personal email-15 pts
42 Agency Insight: The best lead scoring models use a 2-dimensional approach: ICP Fit (are they the right company/person?) + Engagement (are they showing buying intent?). A perfect ICP fit with no engagement shouldn't be an MQL, and high engagement from a poor fit shouldn't be either.
