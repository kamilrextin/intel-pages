SALES ENGAGEMENT COMPARISON

Outreach vs Salesloft vs Apollo

Which sales engagement platform is right for your team size and budget?

Updated April 2026 Based on 100+ implementations

TL;DR - The Quick Answer

Outreach = Enterprise leader. Best AI (Kaia), deepest Salesforce sync. $100-150/user/month.

Salesloft = Balanced platform. Strong conversation intel (Rhythm). $75-125/user/month.

Apollo = All-in-one value. Built-in prospecting data + sequences. $49-99/user/month.

Pick Outreach for enterprise Salesforce shops, Salesloft for mid-market balance, Apollo if you need prospecting data included.

42's Sales Engagement Pick

Apollo for SMB/mid-market. Outreach for enterprise. Salesloft and Outreach are nearly identical—pick based on your CRM (Salesloft integrates slightly better with Salesforce, Outreach with HubSpot). Apollo is the disruptor: 70% of the features at 20% of the cost. For teams under 20 reps, Apollo is the answer.

The Real Differences (42's Practitioner POV)

1.

"Outreach and Salesloft have been feature-matching each other for years."

The winner is whoever gives you the better deal. We've seen 40-60% discounts from both when there's competitive pressure. Play them against each other.

2.

"Apollo's all-in-one approach eliminates integration headaches."

Data + sequences in one platform means no ZoomInfo sync issues, no contact ownership conflicts, no "who enriched what" debates. For lean teams, this matters more than fancy AI features.

3.

"Deliverability is table stakes—all three are fine if you warm domains properly."

We see teams blame their SEP for deliverability issues when the real problem is domain hygiene. Warm your domains, rotate sending accounts, and all three platforms will work fine.

What We Actually Care About

+ What Matters

  • +CRM sync reliability: Does it actually write back to Salesforce/HubSpot without dupes?
  • +Email deliverability infrastructure: Throttling, warmup, domain health monitoring
  • +Analytics that connect to revenue: Can you tie sequences to closed-won deals?
  • +Rep adoption: Will your team actually use it? Complexity kills adoption.

- What's Overhyped

  • -"AI writing": Gimmicks. Your best reps write better than any AI. Train humans.
  • -"Intent data" in SEPs: Buy it separately (6sense, Bombora) if you need it.
  • -"Conversation intelligence": Nice-to-have, not make-or-break for most teams.
  • -"Advanced branching": 90% of sequences are linear. Don't overcomplicate.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Outreach, Salesloft, and Apollo? +

Outreach is the enterprise leader with the most advanced AI (Kaia) and deepest Salesforce integration, priced at $100-150/user/month. Salesloft offers a balanced platform with Rhythm AI and strong conversation intelligence, priced at $75-125/user/month. Apollo is the all-in-one challenger combining prospecting data, sequencing, and dialer at $49-99/user/month, making it ideal for SMBs and startups.

How much does Outreach cost per user? +

Outreach pricing ranges from $100 to $150+ per user per month depending on the tier. Standard tier starts around $100/user/month with core sequencing and analytics. Professional tier runs $125-140/user/month with Kaia AI conversation intelligence. Enterprise tier costs $150+/user/month with advanced forecasting, custom integrations, and dedicated support. Annual contracts are required with minimum seat commitments.

Is Salesloft better than Outreach? +

Neither is universally better - it depends on your needs. Salesloft excels at ease of use, conversation intelligence (via Drift acquisition), and faster implementation. Outreach has stronger enterprise features, deeper Salesforce integration, and more advanced AI with Kaia. Choose Salesloft for mid-market teams wanting balance. Choose Outreach for enterprise sales teams prioritizing AI and Salesforce sync.

Is Apollo good for enterprise sales teams? +

Apollo is best suited for SMB, startup, and mid-market teams rather than enterprise. While Apollo offers excellent value with built-in prospecting data and competitive sequencing at $49-99/user/month, enterprise teams typically need Outreach or Salesloft for deeper CRM integration, compliance controls, advanced AI, and enterprise support SLAs. Consider Apollo if budget is a priority or you need an all-in-one prospecting + engagement platform.

Which sales engagement platform has the best email deliverability? +

All three platforms offer strong deliverability features, but with different approaches. Outreach has the most mature deliverability infrastructure with advanced throttling, domain health monitoring, and dedicated IP options for enterprise. Salesloft offers solid deliverability with smart sending windows and bounce management. Apollo includes email warmup, domain health scores, and sending limits built-in. For high-volume enterprise outreach, Outreach leads. For SMB teams, Apollo's built-in warmup is convenient.
