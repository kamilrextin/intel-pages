ABM PLATFORM COMPARISON

6sense vs Demandbase vs RollWorks

Which ABM platform is right for your company stage and budget?

Updated April 2026 Based on 50+ implementations

TL;DR - The Quick Answer

6sense = Best intent data and predictive AI. Enterprise-grade complexity. $50K-$150K/year.

Demandbase = Most complete platform (ads + intent + sales intel). $50K-$150K/year.

RollWorks = Best value for mid-market. Simpler, faster to deploy. $25K-$60K/year.

Pick 6sense for predictive accuracy, Demandbase for all-in-one ABM, RollWorks if you're not yet at enterprise scale.

42's Take

Most companies don't need a $100K ABM platform.

We've seen too many B2B teams buy 6sense or Demandbase, use 10% of features, and churn after 2 years. Start with intent data (Bombora, G2) + a good enrichment tool (Clay, Clearbit) + your existing CRM. Graduate to full ABM platforms only when you have 10+ reps and dedicated ABM resources.

Based on ABM implementations across 40+ B2B companies

The Uncomfortable Truth

ABM platform vendors sell the dream of "orchestration" but most teams just need better targeting and enrichment. The platforms become expensive shelf-ware.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between 6sense, Demandbase, and RollWorks? +

6sense focuses on AI-powered predictive analytics and intent data aggregation from multiple sources. Demandbase offers the most comprehensive ABM platform with advertising, intent, and sales intelligence in one suite. RollWorks is more affordable and advertising-focused, ideal for mid-market companies starting with ABM. 6sense and Demandbase cost $50K-$150K/year while RollWorks costs $25K-$60K/year.

How much does 6sense cost? +

6sense pricing typically ranges from $50,000 to $150,000+ per year depending on the tier. The Team tier starts around $50K/year for basic intent data and account identification. Growth tier runs $80K-$120K/year with predictive analytics. Enterprise tier costs $120K-$200K+ with full AI features, custom integrations, and dedicated support. Multi-year contracts often include 10-20% discounts.

Is Demandbase better than 6sense? +

Neither is universally better - it depends on your needs. Demandbase excels at integrated advertising and sales intelligence with a more unified platform experience. 6sense has stronger predictive AI and intent data from more diverse sources. Choose Demandbase if you want one platform for ads + intent + sales. Choose 6sense if predictive accuracy and intent aggregation are priorities. Both require similar budgets ($50K-$150K/year).

What is the best ABM platform for startups? +

For startups and early-stage companies, RollWorks is typically the best choice at $25K-$60K/year. It provides solid ABM advertising, account identification, and HubSpot integration without the enterprise complexity of 6sense or Demandbase. Alternatively, consider point solutions like Clearbit for enrichment + LinkedIn Ads for targeting if budget is under $25K/year. Graduate to 6sense or Demandbase when ARR exceeds $20M and you need advanced predictive analytics.

Which ABM platform has the best intent data? +

6sense is generally considered to have the most comprehensive intent data, aggregating signals from multiple third-party sources (Bombora, TrustRadius, G2) plus proprietary first-party data. Demandbase has strong intent data but relies more on its own network. RollWorks partners with Bombora for intent signals but has less proprietary data. For intent data quality specifically, the ranking is: 6sense > Demandbase > RollWorks. However, intent data accuracy varies by industry and use case.
