B2B DATA COMPARISON

ZoomInfo vs Apollo vs Clearbit

Choosing the right B2B data provider for your sales and marketing stack

Updated April 2026 By Kamil Rextin

The Short Answer

ZoomInfo = Enterprise-grade data + intent signals. Best for large sales teams with $15K+ budgets.

Apollo = Data + sequencing in one. Best for startups and SMBs who want all-in-one prospecting.

Clearbit = Marketing enrichment powerhouse. Best for RevOps teams enriching CRM data.

Most B2B companies at scale use two: one for prospecting (ZoomInfo or Apollo) and one for enrichment (Clearbit). The right choice depends on whether you're sales-led or marketing-led.

Need: Contact data, direct dials, email sequences, real-time verification

ZoomInfo (Enterprise) Apollo (SMB)
Why: ZoomInfo has the deepest direct dial coverage and most accurate contact data for enterprise selling. Apollo wins for SMB with its all-in-one data + sequencing at a lower price point.
MARKETING ENRICHMENT

Enriching leads for segmentation and scoring

Need: Firmographics, technographics, company size, industry, funding data

Clearbit
Why: Clearbit has the deepest firmographic and technographic data (100+ attributes), real-time API enrichment, and the best HubSpot/Salesforce integrations for automatic record enrichment.
REVOPS / DATA QUALITY

Need: Real-time enrichment, deduplication, data decay prevention, API access

Clearbit + ZoomInfo
Why: Clearbit excels at real-time enrichment and has a flexible API. Many RevOps teams pair it with ZoomInfo for contact-level data and intent signals that Clearbit lacks.
ABM PROGRAMS

Need: Intent data, buying committees, org charts, account scoring

ZoomInfo
Why: ZoomInfo's native intent data, org charts, and buying committee mapping make it the leader for strategic ABM. Their Scoops and intent signals help prioritize in-market accounts.
STARTUPS / LIMITED BUDGET

Early-stage companies building outbound

Need: Affordable data + outreach, no separate sequencing tool, fast time-to-value

Apollo
Why: Apollo's free tier and $5K starting price is unmatched. Built-in sequencing means no separate Outreach/Salesloft spend. Perfect for 1-5 person sales teams getting started with outbound.

Decision Framework

Answer these questions to find your best fit:

Choose ZoomInfo if:

  • - You have 10+ sales reps doing outbound
  • - You need intent data to prioritize accounts
  • - Direct dials are critical to your motion
  • - Your ACV is $50K+ and you're selling to enterprise
  • - You have $15K+ annual budget for data

Choose Apollo if:

  • - You're a startup or SMB with limited budget
  • - You want data + sequencing in one tool
  • - You have 1-10 sales reps
  • - You need monthly contract flexibility
  • - You're doing high-volume SMB/mid-market outbound

Choose Clearbit if:

  • - Your primary need is CRM data enrichment
  • - You're marketing-led or product-led growth
  • - You need deep firmographic/technographic data for segmentation
  • - You use HubSpot and want the tightest integration
  • - You need real-time form enrichment or website de-anonymization

Use Multiple (ZoomInfo + Clearbit or Apollo + Clearbit) if:

  • - You have both sales prospecting AND marketing enrichment needs
  • - You're mid-market or enterprise with dedicated RevOps
  • - You need intent data (ZoomInfo) AND deep firmographics (Clearbit)
  • - Your budget exceeds $25K/year for data tools

Frequently Asked Questions

Which B2B data provider is best for sales prospecting?

Apollo is best for sales prospecting at SMB/mid-market companies due to its all-in-one approach combining data + sequencing at $5K-$20K/year. ZoomInfo is superior for enterprise sales teams needing premium direct dials and intent data at $15K-$40K/year. Clearbit focuses on marketing enrichment rather than prospecting, so it's not ideal for outbound sales use cases.

How accurate is ZoomInfo vs Apollo vs Clearbit email data?

ZoomInfo reports 85-90% email accuracy with human verification processes. Apollo claims 80-85% accuracy with real-time verification. Clearbit focuses on firmographic enrichment rather than email discovery, so email accuracy isn't their primary metric. For cold outbound, ZoomInfo leads in email deliverability; for inbound enrichment, Clearbit leads in firmographic depth.

What is the pricing difference between ZoomInfo, Apollo, and Clearbit?

ZoomInfo costs $15,000-$40,000/year depending on seats and data access. Apollo ranges from $5,000-$20,000/year with more flexible seat-based pricing. Clearbit pricing is $12,000-$30,000/year based on monthly enrichment volume. Apollo offers the best value for startups, ZoomInfo for enterprise teams, and Clearbit for marketing-focused enrichment.

Can I use ZoomInfo, Apollo, or Clearbit with HubSpot?

All three integrate with HubSpot. Clearbit has the deepest native HubSpot integration, automatically enriching records with 100+ firmographic attributes. ZoomInfo offers a robust bi-directional sync. Apollo provides basic CRM sync plus its own sequencing platform. For HubSpot-centric RevOps, Clearbit is typically the cleanest integration.

Should I choose ZoomInfo, Apollo, or Clearbit for ABM?

For ABM, ZoomInfo excels with intent data, org charts, and buying committee mapping. Clearbit is strong for enriching target account lists with technographic and firmographic data. Apollo is better suited for high-volume prospecting than strategic ABM. For enterprise ABM programs, ZoomInfo + Clearbit together cover intent signals and enrichment depth.
