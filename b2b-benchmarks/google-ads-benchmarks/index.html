42 Agency Insight: Exact match keywords deliver 2x better cost per MQL than phrase match ($1,200 vs $2,800). For B2B, start with exact match on high-intent terms like "[product] demo," "[product] pricing," and "[competitor] alternative" before expanding to phrase match.
