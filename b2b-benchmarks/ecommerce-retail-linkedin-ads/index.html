Why Meta Works for B2B Retail

Unlike most B2B verticals, e-commerce and retail tech sees strong performance on Meta. Retail and e-commerce professionals are active on Instagram and Facebook, and the visual nature of retail products makes these platforms effective for B2B messaging.

Key insight: Meta CPLs of $50-$100 are 3-5x lower than typical B2B LinkedIn CPLs. The trade-off is lead quality—expect a longer nurture cycle from Meta leads.
