The Enterprise Premium in FinTech

FinTech has one of the largest gaps between enterprise and SMB CPL. Targeting companies with 50+ employees drives CPL up 5-7x because you're competing with well-funded competitors (Brex, Ramp, Airbase) who have raised billions and can outspend on ads.

The solution: Use content-first strategies to build pipeline at $200-$400 CPL, then nurture to sales-ready. Direct lead gen at enterprise is only viable for bottom-funnel retargeting.
