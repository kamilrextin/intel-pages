The Gift Card Strategy

LinkedIn InMail is expensive—$0.50-$1.50 per send. Without a compelling hook, response rates are 3-8% and CPL often exceeds $1,500. But with a $100 Amazon gift card offer for a 30-minute call, we consistently see 60-70% CPL reduction across industries.

The economics work: $100 gift card + ~$100 in InMail sends = $200 all-in cost for a qualified conversation. Compare that to $1,500+ CPL with standard approaches.

42 Agency Insight: The $100 gift card isn't bribery—it's value exchange. You're asking busy executives for 30 minutes of their time. The gift card communicates "we respect your time and have budget to invest in relationships." It also pre-qualifies: people who schedule calls for $100 are genuinely interested, not just looking for free money.
