Why MarTech/SalesTech Is Competitive

Marketing and sales professionals are among the most targeted audiences on LinkedIn. Every MarTech vendor is competing for the same CMOs, VPs of Marketing, and RevOps leaders. This drives up CPLs but also means these audiences are highly engaged with content.

Key insight: MarTech buyers are sophisticated about marketing tactics—they'll recognize lazy campaigns immediately. Original research, unique data, and contrarian takes outperform generic "best practices" content.
