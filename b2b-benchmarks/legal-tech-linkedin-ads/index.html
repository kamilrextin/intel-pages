Why Legal Tech Has Lower CPLs

Legal tech consistently achieves some of the lowest CPLs in B2B because of highly specific targeting. Paralegals, litigation support managers, and legal ops professionals are well-defined audiences on LinkedIn with less competition than broader tech categories.

Key success factor: Role-specific content. A "Paralegal Playbook" performs 3x better than a generic "Legal Tech Guide" because it speaks directly to the end user, not just the buyer.

42 Agency Insight: The biggest mistake in legal tech LinkedIn campaigns is targeting too broadly. "Legal professionals" includes 2M+ people. Narrow to specific roles (Paralegal, Litigation Support, Legal Ops Director) and company types (AmLaw 200, Corporate Legal Departments) to cut CPL by 40-60%.
