Why Google Search Outperforms for SQLs

Logistics and supply chain software sees high Google Search CPLs ($1,500-$3,000) but these leads are significantly more qualified. Buyers actively searching for "TMS software" or "warehouse management system" are often in active buying cycles, not just researching.

Key insight: LinkedIn works best for top-of-funnel awareness and nurture, while Google Search captures bottom-funnel intent. A blended strategy using both channels typically outperforms single-channel approaches.
