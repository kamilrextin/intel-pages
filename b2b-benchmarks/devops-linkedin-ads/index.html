The Developer Marketing Challenge

Developers are notoriously ad-resistant. They use ad blockers, ignore promotional content, and distrust vendor messaging. Standard LinkedIn campaigns targeting developers produce some of the worst CPLs in B2B—often $1,500-$2,500 per lead.

The breakthrough: Amazon gift cards. A $100-$105 Amazon gift card attached to an InMail requesting a 30-minute call achieves 46% open rates and $265-$422 CPL—a 70%+ reduction from standard approaches.

Important: Don't run in-feed lead gen forms for developer audiences. The CPL will be $2,000-$2,500. Developers don't fill out forms—they research independently. InMail with direct value exchange (gift card for conversation) is the only scalable approach.

The gift card signals "we respect your time" and converts passive scrollers into active responders. ROI is typically 4-5x the gift card cost.
