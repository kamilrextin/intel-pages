Why UCaaS Has Moderate CPLs

UCaaS and telecom see efficient CPLs because IT decision makers are well-defined on LinkedIn and actively search for solutions on Google. The market is mature with established buying patterns, making both channels effective.

Key insight: TCO (Total Cost of Ownership) calculators and ROI tools drive high engagement. IT buyers want to build a business case—give them the tools to do it.
