Why Privacy & Security Performs Well

Privacy and security software benefits from regulatory tailwinds. New legislation (EU AI Act, state privacy laws, SEC cyber rules) creates urgency that generic B2B campaigns lack. Topical content tied to specific regulations consistently outperforms evergreen material.

Key success factor: Timing campaigns around regulatory deadlines. A "CPRA Compliance Checklist" released 90 days before enforcement outperforms the same content 6 months later by 3-4x on CPL.

42 Agency Insight: Privacy and security buyers are highly skeptical of vendor content. Position thought leadership around the regulation, not your product. "What the EU AI Act Means for Your Data Strategy" works; "How [Product] Helps with EU AI Act Compliance" doesn't.
