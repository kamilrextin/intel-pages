Why Healthcare Has Higher CPLs

Healthcare tech faces a perfect storm of high CPLs: highly regulated buying processes, long sales cycles (6-18 months), and limited LinkedIn penetration among clinical staff. Hospital IT and administrative roles are reachable, but clinical decision-makers often aren't active on LinkedIn.

The solution: InMail with gift card incentives. Spa and wellness gift cards resonate particularly well with healthcare professionals, achieving 40-45% open rates and cutting CPL by 60%.

42 Agency Insight: In healthcare, the gift card isn't just a bribe—it's a signal of respect for their time. Healthcare professionals are notoriously over-scheduled. A $50-$75 spa card acknowledges this reality and converts skeptics into responders. ROI is typically 3-4x the gift card cost.
