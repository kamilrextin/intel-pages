Why EdTech/HR Tech Has Moderate CPLs

EdTech and HR Tech benefit from well-defined buyer personas (L&D Directors, CHROs, HR Managers) who are active on LinkedIn. Unlike broader tech categories, these audiences self-identify through their job titles, making targeting efficient.

Key insight: Seasonal timing matters. Q1 (budget planning) and Q3 (pre-annual review) see higher engagement and conversion rates for HR Tech. EdTech peaks in Q2-Q3 for back-to-school planning.
